# CELTA Prep - Product Marketing Context

## Product Overview

**Name:** CELTA Prep  
**Website:** www.celtaprep.com  
**Type:** Online CELTA preparation course + free resources

## What We Offer

**Free Resources:**
- "Think Like a CELTA Trainer" booklet
- "See Your Learner" booklet
- "CELTA Mindset" booklet
- Lesson plan templates
- Assignment frameworks

**Premium Course - CELTA Unlocked:**
- 8-module video course
- Assignment templates with examples
- Live Q&A sessions
- Email support during CELTA
- Price: $147-197 (launch pricing TBD)

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Moroccan English teachers preparing for CELTA  
**Secondary:** English teachers globally doing CELTA

**Customer Profile:**
- Age: 25-45
- Teaching in private schools or language centers in Morocco
- Want to upgrade credentials with CELTA
- Working full-time, time-limited
- Anxious about CELTA difficulty

**Main Pain Points:**
1. "CELTA is overwhelming - what do I focus on?"
2. "I don't know how to think like a CELTA trainer"
3. "The assignments are confusing"
4. "I'm afraid I'll fail"
5. "I don't have time to prepare properly while working"

**Language They Use:**
- "CELTA pass rate"
- "How hard is CELTA?"
- "CELTA preparation"
- "Teaching practice tips"
- "Assignment help"
- "Will I pass CELTA?"

## Value Proposition

**Main Message:**  
Pass CELTA first time with confidence - even if you're working full-time.

**Why We're Different:**
1. Created by CELTA + DELTA + Train the Trainers certified instructor
2. Designed specifically for Moroccan teaching context
3. Focus on mindset + strategy, not just templates
4. Real trainer perspective (not just someone who passed)
5. Cross-cultural experience (Morocco + Vietnam)

## Proof Points

**Creator Credentials:**
- CELTA certified
- DELTA Module 2 in progress (Distinction level)
- Train the Trainers qualified
- BA in Linguistics + TESOL certified
- 5+ years training teachers in Vietnam
- Understands both Moroccan and international teaching contexts

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Voice Characteristics:**
- Direct and practical (no fluff)
- Empathetic but not hand-holding
- Expert but accessible
- Moroccan-context aware
- Confident and encouraging

**Avoid:**
- Overpromising ("Guaranteed to pass!")
- Condescending language
- Generic platitudes
- Corporate jargon

## Business Model

**Funnel:**
1. Free booklet download (lead magnet)
2. 5-day email challenge
3. Premium course offer
4. Optional: Live cohort (future)

**Marketing Channels:**
- Facebook groups (Moroccan ELT teachers)
- Organic Facebook content
- Word of mouth
- Future: Facebook ads, YouTube

## Competition

**Direct Competitors:**
- CELTA course center prep materials (usually basic)
- Random YouTube videos (inconsistent quality)

**Our Advantage:**
- Niche focus on CELTA specifically
- Moroccan context awareness
- Comprehensive and structured
- Real trainer expertise

## Current Stage

**Status:** Fresh launch - zero customers yet  
**Goal:** Get first 20-50 beta customers, collect testimonials, prove concept  
**Timeline:** Launching March 2026
